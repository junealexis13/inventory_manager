Steps in adding products to your inventory

  1. Create a category - Product category should be assigned for each product type. This is helpful especially for categorizing large inventory.
  2. Define Supplier Information - embedding supplier information is also vital for us to take note particularly if we want to report defective products or any problems that can be addressed by the supplier.
  3. List down product information - register your product information here. Any information including the technical specifications and pricing are vital.
  4. Inventory Status - now that you have successfully defined all product information, you can now list down inventory informations using all the schema provided earlier.
